## Quick Facts for Voice Assistant

### Platform Name
CuraLink

### Platform Purpose
Connecting patients with clinical trials and medical experts for better healthcare outcomes

### Available Features
- Browse clinical trials by specialty
- Connect with medical experts
- AI-powered chatbot for health questions
- Voice assistant for hands-free interaction
- Save favorite trials and experts

### Supported Medical Specialties
- Oncology (Cancer)
- Cardiology (Heart)
- Neurology (Brain & Nerves)
- Endocrinology (Diabetes & Hormones)
- Respiratory Medicine (Lungs)
- Immunology (Immune System)

### How to Get Started
1. Sign up or log in to the platform
2. Complete your health profile
3. Browse available trials and experts
4. Connect with AI assistant for personalized recommendations
5. Save items of interest and apply to trials
